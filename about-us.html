Continuing the legacy of 125 years, HERSHEY'S forays into India with its impeccable and irresistible assortment of flavors with chocolates, candy and beverages.

Built on compassion, purpose, and selflessness, our founder, Milton Hershey has left behind a legacy of delectable treats. Every bite of a HERSHEY'S assortment is decadent and of superior quality, that transforms your regular snacking moments into blissful bonding experiences. HERSHEY India Private Ltd. is a 100% subsidiary of The HERSHEY Company, a 100+ year old company based out of Hershey, PA. It is the largest producer of rich, exotic and quality chocolates in North America and a global leader known for bringing happiness to the world through its chocolates, candy, beverages and other irresistible delights. The company has more than 80 brands globally, including iconic brands like HERSHEY'S KISSES, EXOTIC DARK, SOFIT, JOLLY RANCHER, and NUTRINE
